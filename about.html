I'm Diego Rago, a pixel artist, character designer, and comic artist from Argentina.

I'm drawn to work with strong silhouettes, expressive motion, and worlds that feel lived-in. Whether it's a combat animation, a strange little prop, or a quiet environment, I like images that communicate personality immediately.

My background in comics shapes the way I approach game art: timing, clarity, rhythm, and storytelling matter as much as rendering. Even a small sprite should feel intentional.

I'm currently available for selected freelance collaborations in games, animation, and visual development.
